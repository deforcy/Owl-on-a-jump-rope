This mod adds three items (for now), all found in their own **Owl on a Jump Rope** creative tab:
- **Owl on a jumping rope card** - the horseman of death.
- **Uno Reverse Card** - your best friend
- **Deck of cards** (8 uses) - drops an "owl on a jump rope" card in front of you.
